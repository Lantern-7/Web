Welcome to “Are You a Human?” Fun Test

This interactive web app is a fun logic challenge designed to test your responses and determine whether you are human, a robot, or something in between. You will answer a few simple questions, and based on your choices, then the system will analyze your answers and reveal your result.

Think carefully, answer honestly, and enjoy the challenge.
